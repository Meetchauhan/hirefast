WHAT IS HIREFAST?

Hirefast is an advanced technology recruiting platform focused on software engineers and information technology professionals and makes highly accurate and relevant matches much faster than traditional approaches.

Hirefast starts with repositories of STEM professionals' shared work product and uses automation and advanced data augmentation techniques to assess their demonstrated capabilities.

Current State of Affairs

Non-technical recruiters struggle to match talented technology professionals with the right opportunities.

Passive and active candidates receive bland, off-the-mark solicitations; primarily via email and InMails on LinkedIn.

REINVENTING TECH RECRUITING

At Hirefast, we seek to revolutionize the way talent is sourced; both in terms of how candidates are found and how they are communicated to and engaged with. Hirefast is data AND human-centric.

At Hirefast, we seek to start with repositories and platforms that retain demonstrable contributions, intentions and talent and combine that with other platform data, including LinkedIn.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to markedly improve the efficiency, cost, and experience for candidates, hiring companies and talent acquisition participants alike, by developing and bringing to market human and data-centric technology solutions to market.

PROBLEM

The current state of talent acquisition is isn't serving companies, agencies or candidates. In fact, it is broken. Alternative approaches and tools are desparately needed to improve the process of filling open positions with the right people.

WHY HIREFAST?

ENRICHED DATA

Github is the largest public repository of software components. Augmenting this profile data with other publicly available information and algorithmically scoring contributions allows Hirefast to assign a relative score to other similar profiles helping recruiters to find better talent, in markedly less time.

NL SEARCH

Hirefast is an early adopter of Open AI ChatGPT, which enables users to find candidates in specific locations, with numerous desired skills without having to write complex boolean search strings.

360° CONFIDENCE

Hirefast can perform a 360 degree assessment of a profile by combining Github ratings, Hirefast tech scoring and other publicly available social media and community participation; resulting in high levels of confidence for matching candidates and job requirements.
